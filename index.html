The Velvet Room is the perfect environment for a comfortable, spacious and collaborative session. Nestled in the heart of Koreatown, with 800+ sqft, this is truly a unique and open space to create!

Equipped with industry standard gear, fully acoustic treated acoustic playback room and comfy vocal booth.

Catch a vibe with our massive projector, kitchen area, mood lighting, widescreen TVs, velvet flooring, large couches and more!

Our engineers and producers are on standby.
